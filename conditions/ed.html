Erectile dysfunction.

A clinical assessment, a registered clinician, and a treatment plan that actually works. All handled privately and delivered to your door.

The problem

ED is common. Leaving it untreated isn't necessary.

Around 1 in 5 New Zealand men experience erectile dysfunction at some point. Most don't seek help. Not because they don't want to, but because the path to treatment hasn't been built for how men actually make decisions about things like this.

ED is also frequently a signal. Low testosterone, cardiovascular changes, sleep issues, and stress all show up here first. A proper clinical assessment doesn't just treat the symptom.
